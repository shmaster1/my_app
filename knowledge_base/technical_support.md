# Technical Support / App Usage

1. Creating an Account:
   - Click "Sign Up" on the main page.
   - Enter full name, email, username, password, phone, city, country.
   - All fields are mandatory.

2. Logging In:
   - Click "Login" and enter your credentials.

3. Placing an Order:
   - Browse items in the dashboard catalog.
   - Add desired items to your cart.
   - Go to orders page, update your cart if needed and confirm payment.

4. User Settings:
   - Navigate to settings page.
   - Users can delete their account.
   - Deleting removes orders, favorites, and other personal data.
   - Deleted users cannot recover their account.

5. Refund / Shipping Policy:
   - Refunds are not available.
   - Items can only be removed from the cart (temp order).
   - Shipping address is currently our main office.
   - There are no shipping fees.
