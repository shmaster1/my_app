# Product / Item Guide

1. Product Categories:
   - Fruits and vegetables

2. Item Details:
   - Each product / item shows total price and current stock availability.
   - Out-of-stock items will be removed from the dashboard.
   - Users can pick a quantity from the amount dropdown on the item card.

3. Reviews & Ratings:
   - Users cannot rate or leave reviews on products.

4. Cart & Orders:
   - Items can be removed from the cart (temp order) before confirming purchase.
   - Temporary orders are editable; historical orders are read-only.
   - Out-of-stock items are marked in the temp order.
